## Long Description Draft
MySky is a private self-pattern tracker for sleep, mood, dreams, and relationships.

Log how you feel, how you sleep, what you dream, and what keeps showing up in your inner world. Over time, MySky turns that private history into clearer signals: weekly shifts, recurring themes, and guidance that gets more personal as your record grows.

**Track your patterns**
- Daily check-ins for mood, energy, and sleep quality
- Dream journal with symbolic interpretation and a recurring symbol cluster map
- Keyword lift — see what words appear on your best vs hardest days
- Emotion tone shifts across your full journal history
- What restores you, drains you, or shows up during heavier days

**Know your identity more deeply**
- *Inner World* — explore your core values, dominant archetypes, and cognitive style through short daily reflections
- *Relational Mirror* — map your relational gravity: where you move toward, pull away, or hold rigid in connection
- *Body & Nervous System* — somatic map for tracking where emotions live in your body, plus a trigger log that builds your nervous system profile over time
- *Inner Tensions* *(Deeper Sky)* — maps the specific places in your chart where drives conflict: ambivalence signals, nervous system states, dream patterns, and shadow triggers — all computed locally, nothing transmitted

**Understand yourself more deeply** *(Deeper Sky)*
- Behavioral trend charts: sleep and mood over time
- Healing and inner work: attachment style themes, fear patterns, shadow work
- Personalized daily guidance with key insights, emotional weather, and evening reflections
- Chiron sensitivity mapping with body awareness cues
- 10 emotional chapters of your personal story: how you love, protect, and grow

**Relationships**
- Compare your chart with a partner, friend, child, or parent
- See how you truly connect — your emotional dynamic, not just compatibility scores

**Private by design**
- Core reflections are encrypted on-device
- Encrypted backup and restore with your own passphrase
- Your data never leaves your device unless you choose to export it

MySky is for people who want a deeper relationship with their own patterns, not just one-time insight. The more you use it, the more useful it becomes.

## ASO Notes
- Lead with sleep, mood, dreams, and patterns before astrology.
- Use privacy language as a differentiator, not just a compliance note.
- Keep the promise longitudinal: over time, weekly shifts, recurring themes.
- Avoid trying to sell every feature in the first sentence.
- "Deeper Sky" is the premium tier — use it in the description to name the upgrade, not just hint at it.
- Keyword lift, emotion tone shifts, and dream symbol clustering are real differentiators — name them explicitly.
