## Long Description Draft
MySky is a private self-pattern tracker for sleep, mood, dreams, and relationships.

Track how you feel, how you sleep, what you dream, and what repeats in your inner world. Over time, MySky turns your private history into clearer signals: weekly shifts, recurring themes, and more personal guidance.

Use MySky to:
- Log sleep, mood, energy, and daily reflections
- Journal dreams and notice recurring symbols or emotional patterns
- See what changed this week and what keeps repeating
- Understand what restores you, drains you, or shows up during heavier days
- Explore relationship and self-reflection patterns over time
- Keep your core reflections private and encrypted on-device

MySky is designed for people who want a deeper relationship with their own patterns, not just one-time insight. The more you use it, the more useful it becomes.
