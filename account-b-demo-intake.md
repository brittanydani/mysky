## Trigger / Glimmer Events

Use these for nervous-system events. Aim for 8 to 15 entries.

For each entry, fill:
- Date and time
- Mode (`drain` or `nourish`)
- Event title
- Nervous system state (`ventral_vagal`, `sympathetic`, `dorsal_vagal`)
- Sensations
- Note

Quick guide:
- `nourish`: glimmers, safety, connection, relief, joy, regulation
- `drain`: triggers, overwhelm, depletion, activation, shutdown
- `ventral_vagal`: safe, grounded, open, connected
- `sympathetic`: anxious, activated, urgent, irritated
- `dorsal_vagal`: numb, heavy, shut down, disconnected
